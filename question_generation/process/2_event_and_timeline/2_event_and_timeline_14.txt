From May 10, 2020 to May 11, 2020, the Wuhan Health Commission notified six new cases, all of which came from the Sanmin neighborhood on Changqing Street in the East-West Lake District of Wuhan, which led to the reclosure of the neighborhood. On the evening of May 11, 2020, Wuhan City issued an "Urgent Notice on the City's New Coronavirus Nucleic Acid Screening", which required all neighborhoods in the city to conduct universal testing for up to ten days. from May 12, 2020, the neighborhoods in Wuhan City began their respective neighborhoods' universal testing for up to ten days one after another.
