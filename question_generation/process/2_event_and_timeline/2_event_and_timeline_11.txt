April 2020
At a media briefing on the outbreak on April 1, 2020, WHO Director-General Desai Tan expressed deep concern about the rapid escalation of infections and their global spread. In the past five weeks, the number of new cases has increased almost exponentially, in almost every country, territory and area, said Tan Desai. In the past week, the number of deaths has more than doubled. In the next few days, it will reach one million confirmed cases and 50,000 deaths. Although relatively few confirmed cases have been reported in Africa, Central America and South America, COVID-19 could have serious social, economic and political consequences for these regions.
