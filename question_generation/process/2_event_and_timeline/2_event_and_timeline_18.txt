On September 11, 2020, the United Nations General Assembly adopted a resolution on the response to the 2019 coronavirus disease outbreak by a vote of 169 in favor and 2 against (the United States and Israel). The resolution covers multiple aspects of the new coronavirus outbreak, including "recognition of the key leadership role of the WHO and the fundamental role of the United Nations system in promoting and coordinating a comprehensive global response to the epidemic. The resolution calls for "enhanced international cooperation and solidarity to contain, mitigate and overcome the epidemic and its consequences," and supports the UN Secretary-General's Global Ceasefire Initiative, while calling for "the immediate removal of unjustifiable obstacles.
