5. The presence of drowsiness and convulsions.

(iv) Critical type.
Those who meet one of the following conditions.
1. the presence of respiratory failure and the need for mechanical ventilation.
2. The presence of shock.
3. The combination of other organ failure requires ICU monitoring treatment.
VII. Heavy/critical high-risk groups
(i) Older people older than 60 years old.
(ii) Those with underlying diseases such as cardiovascular diseases (including hypertension), chronic lung diseases, diabetes, chronic liver and kidney diseases, and tumors.
